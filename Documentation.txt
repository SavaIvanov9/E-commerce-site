Automatic start:
   -Edit LaunchConfiguration/Configuration.txt 
	1.Write at first line location of mongod.exe
	2.Write at second line location of mongodata folder
	3.Write at third line the name of project folder

Manual Start:
1.Run Mongo from CMD:
	C:\Program Files\MongoDB\Server\3.4\bin>mongod.exe
	--dbpath F:/Development/MongoData
2.Run app.js
